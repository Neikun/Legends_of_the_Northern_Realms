Player Party Options Poll:
Player must not be allowed to use the default party. This is already decided upon due to the timeline of this mod.

1. Player uses preset characters created by us. Player can recruit/remove party members during play in the mod.

2. Player uses preset characters created by us. Player cannot recruit/remove party members during play in the mod.

3. Player is restricted to making one character. All other party slots will be filled by recruitable/dismissable NPCs met in game.

4. Player is restricted to making one character. No other characters will be added to the party.

5. Player is allowed to make a fully custom party (1-4 slots).  No other characters will be added to the party.

6. Player is allowed to make a fully custom party(1-4 slots). Player will be allowed to recruit/dismiss characters started with and found in game.
